Return Policy for Bankapur Shirting and Suiting

Introduction

Welcome to the Return Policy for Bankapur Shirting and Suiting. This policy outlines the terms and conditions for returning products purchased from our website.

Eligibility for Returns

  • The item must be unused, unworn, and in the same condition as received.
  • It must be returned within 30 days of the purchase date.
  • The original receipt or proof of purchase must be provided.

Process for Returns

  1. Contact our customer service team at bankapurfashion@gmail.com to initiate the return process.
  2. Package the item securely, including any accessories, documentation, and original packaging.
  3. Ship the item to the address provided by our customer service team.

Refunds

Upon receiving and inspecting the returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed, and a credit will be applied to your original method of payment within a specified number of days.
